Our Story

The Electric Cooler Co. is a family business with more than 30 years of cooler‑building experience. Our journey started in 1991, when my mother — after 15 years designing coolers for other companies — said, “Why don’t we build our own?” I had just finished college, and without hesitation I said, “I’m in.”

Together, we built what became the largest ice barrel cooler company in the world. As the industry shifted, we shifted with it — first manufacturing in the U.S., then partnering with Mexico during the early NAFTA years, and eventually opening our own factory in China to maintain the quality and pricing our customers expected.

Through every chapter, one thing stayed the same: our commitment to designing coolers that perform, last, and make brands look good.

Since 2010, we’ve focused on electric refrigerated coolers built to U.S. standards, engineered with the durability and efficiency that major brands rely on. And now, more than three decades later, we’re proud to introduce our newest design: the EBC‑165‑LED — a modern, electric barrel cooler built for today’s retail environments.

The Electric Cooler Co. is a division of Excelenté International Refrigeración (www.ExIntRef.com).

— Greg, Founder & President
